About Mahdi

Introducing Mahdi Sultan

Mahdi Sultan, also known by his birth name Sultan Ahmed Foyzul Islam, is a trailblazer in the Bangladeshi music scene, captivating audiences with his soulful melodies and romantic ballads.

This acclaimed singer-songwriter rose to global prominence with his debut single, "Tor Mon Paray," released on July 13, 2018, which swiftly became a hit, garnering over 162 million views on YouTube.

Renowned for his ability to blend contemporary Bengali pop with traditional sounds, Sultan's music offers an authentic local flavor, capturing the hearts of audiences through soaring soulful melodies.

Proficient in multiple languages—Bengali, Hindi, Urdu, Punjabi, and English—his cultural versatility enhances his global appeal.

Historic Achievement

"Tor Mon Paray" became the 2nd most-viewed Bengali song globally, achieving over 162 million views organically from a zero-subscriber YouTube channel start - making it the highest-viewed debut content from a new channel in the Bengali music genre.
